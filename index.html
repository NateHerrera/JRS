About Our Service

In the petroleum industry, numerous underground and above-ground fuel storage tanks are required by state regulations and insurance policies to have secondary containment systems. A flexible membrane excavation liner is often recommended due to its low cost and ability to handle ground movement, which can cause rigid containers to leak. This also applies to the extensive piping connected to these storage systems. In addition, the storage of chemicals, fertilizers, and other hazardous substances in the industry must have both primary and secondary containment systems.

HighLine 610 is a 1:1, fast setting, spray applied two component 100% polyurea coating. It is 100% solid and contains zero VOC's. HighLine 610 is specifically designed for application to geotextile fabric to form a durable, flexible, seamless liner for use in secondary containment applications. It is ideal for preventing leaks and containing spills of wastewater, fuels, and many other chemicals. HighLine 610 has exceptional resistance to impact (cold) and chemical attack.
