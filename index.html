About Our Service

In the petroleum industry, numerous underground and above-ground fuel storage tanks are required by state regulations and insurance policies to have secondary containment systems. A flexible membrane excavation liner is often recommended due to its low cost and ability to handle ground movement, which can cause rigid containers to leak. This also applies to the extensive piping connected to these storage systems. In addition, the storage of chemicals, fertilizers, and other hazardous substances in the industry must have both primary and secondary containment systems. Polyurethane flexible membrane liners are commonly used for this purpose, exceeding regulatory standards. Polyurethane is highly flexible, puncture-resistant, and offers excellent heat-sealing properties. It is resistant to UV degradation and underground fungi, ensuring long-lasting durability for underground fuel and chemical storage.
