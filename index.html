About Us

Jaime's Roustabout Service (JRS Repairs) specializes in advanced containment solutions designed to meet the rigorous demands of the petroleum and chemical industries. Our mission is to provide reliable and effective systems that help companies safely manage and store hazardous materials.

About Our Service

In industries like petroleum and chemical manufacturing, safety and environmental responsibility are essential. We install secondary containment systems that provide an extra layer of protection against spills and leaks, helping companies stay compliant with regulations and avoid costly incidents. Our solutions, like flexible membrane liners, are ideal for both underground and above-ground storage tanks, enabling them to withstand ground shifts while providing robust containment.

One of our premium offerings, HighLine 610, is a high-performance, spray-applied polyurea coating. This fast-curing, durable liner is designed for easy application to geotextile fabric, creating a seamless and flexible barrier. HighLine 610 is especially suited for containing wastewater, fuel, and various chemicals, offering outstanding resistance to physical and chemical damage. With zero VOCs, it's also an environmentally safe choice for effective secondary containment.

Comprehensive Repair Services

Our repair team is equipped to handle a wide range of containment issues, from membrane repairs to structural reinforcements. Whether it's sealing a small leak or replacing an entire liner, we use top-quality materials and advanced techniques to restore your containment systems to peak performance. Our repair services minimize downtime and extend the lifespan of your equipment, providing a reliable and cost-effective solution.
